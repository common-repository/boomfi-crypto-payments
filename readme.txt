The BoomFi Crypto Payments Plugin enables e-commerce stores to effortlessly accept cryptocurrency payments through WooCommerce.

== Description ==

The [BoomFi](https://www.boomfi.xyz/) Crypto Payments Plugin enables e-commerce stores to effortlessly accept cryptocurrency payments through WooCommerce.

## Key Features

- **Major Crypto Support:** Accept leading cryptocurrencies like USDC, USDT, DAI, and ETH across major blockchains.
- **Settle in crypto or fiat**: Choose to settle your payments to your crypto wallet or bank.
- **Fiat Settlement**: Major currencies via SEPA, FPS, SWIFT, ACH, and Wire.
- **24/7 Support:** Get help anytime via chat or email.
- **Fast Onboarding:** Get your merchant approved fast with online KYB/KYC.

## Benefits of Accepting Crypto Payments

- **Expand Your Reach:** Access the growing crypto market with millions of users worldwide.
- **Fast Settlements:** Get paid quickly across borders in either crypto or fiat.
- **No Chargebacks:** Reduce fraud risk with secure, irreversible transactions.
- **Global Access:** Connect with customers worldwide without intermediaries.
- **Lower Fees:** Save on costs compared to traditional payment methods. You can also pass these savings on to your customers.

## Supported Networks and Cryptocurrencies

BoomFi supports popular blockchains such as Ethereum, Solana, Tron, and Polygon, making it easy for you to accept crypto payments. You can receive payments in widely used cryptocurrencies like USDC, USDT, DAI, and ETH, among others.

## Getting Started

To add BoomFi Crypto Payments to your WooCommerce store, you'll need:

1. **WooCommerce:** Install and activate WooCommerce on your WordPress site.
2. **Crypto Wallets:** You can bring your wallet (self-custody) or have us provision a wallet for you (custody).
3. **BoomFi Account:** Sign up on BoomFi to complete your setup and start accepting crypto payments.
4. **Complete KYB:** This is required to unlock fiat features as per our Virtual Asset Service Provider license.

For a step by step guide, checkout our [demo](https://app.supademo.com/demo/cm29eemn20m92vm5s80gb8279?v_email=EMAIL)

For a detailed walkthrough, check out our [quick setup guide](https://blog.boomfi.xyz/how-to-accept-crypto-payments-on-your-woocommerce-store-with-the-boomfi-crypto-payments-plugin/).

BoomFi Group is a licensed Virtual Asset Service Provider licensed in Poland.

## Prerequisites

1. WordPress hosting to host the woocommerce based eCommerce
2. Install, enable and configure [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) plugin
3. Wallet accounts
   1. EVM based blockchain accounts (Polygon, Arbitrium One, Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Base) using [Metamask](https://metamask.io/download/) or something similar. Add EVM based networks (Polygon, Arbitrium One, Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, and Base) and import the tokens (USDC, USDT, DAI and WETH)
   2. Tron wallet account [TronLink](https://www.tronlink.org/), or something similar
   3. Solana wallet account [Phantom](https://phantom.app/en-GB), or something similar
4. An account at BoomFi, signup at ([Test](https://test.boomfi.xyz/signup), [Live](https://app.boomfi.xyz/signup)) and complete KYB process
